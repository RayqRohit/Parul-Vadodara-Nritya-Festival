About Parul Institute of Performing Arts (PIPA)

Parul Institute of Performing Arts (PIPA) stands as a beacon of excellence, nurturing the artistic prowess of students through a rich, interdisciplinary curriculum in music, dance, drama, and literature. With a holistic focus, PIPA fosters creativity and cultural understanding while providing rigorous training under acclaimed faculty, shaping the next generation of artists and cultural leaders.

  • Interdisciplinary courses in Music, Dance, Drama, Literature
  • Industry and global exposure through events & workshops
  • Personalized training & mentorship by esteemed faculty
  • Dedicated to social responsibility and cultural heritage
