About Vadodara Rashtriya Natya Mahotsav

Vadodara Rashtriya Natya Mahotsav is a grand celebration of the performing arts, dedicated to honoring the vibrant traditions and evolving expressions of Indian theatre. This dynamic festival brings together artists, performers, and theatre enthusiasts from across Gujarat and the country, creating a shared space for storytelling, cultural exchange, and artistic excellence. Hosted by Parul University, the festival is a platform for traditional and contemporary forms of theatre. Scheduled for 19th to 22nd August at Parul University, Vadodara, it features performances by renowned groups, ensembles, and artists, along with celebrated figures like Rakesh Bedi, Himani Shivpuri, Saurabh Shukla, Naseeruddin Shah, and Ratna Pathak Shah. Vadodara Rashtriya Natya Mahotsav is more than just a festival‚Äîit‚Äôs a cultural movement to nurture talent, celebrate diversity, and build a thriving theatre ecosystem for the future.
