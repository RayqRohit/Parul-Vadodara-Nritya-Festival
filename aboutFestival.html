About Festival

19th – 22nd August 2025 | Parul University, Vadodara

About Incredible Vadodara Rashtriya Natya Mahotsav Cultural Festival

Rashtriya Natya Mahotsav is a grand celebration of the performing arts, dedicated to honoring the vibrant traditions and evolving expressions of Indian theatre. This dynamic and inclusive festival is envisioned as a platform that brings together artists, performers, and theatre enthusiasts from across Gujarat and the country, creating a shared space for storytelling, cultural exchange, and artistic excellence.

Hosted by Parul University, a NAAC A++ accredited institution known for its strong commitment to academic innovation and cultural advancement, the festival reflects the university's dedication to nurturing creativity and promoting the performing arts as an essential part of holistic education. With its emphasis on both traditional and contemporary forms of theatre, Rashtriya Natya Mahotsav marks a significant milestone in the cultural journey of the state.

Scheduled to take place from 19th to 22nd August at the beautiful campus of Parul University, Vadodara, Gujarat, the festival will feature an exciting array of performances by leading theatre groups, university ensembles, and independent artists. Each day of the event promises a vibrant mix of genres, languages, and styles, offering audiences an immersive experience into the world of drama and live performance.

Adding to the grandeur of the festival, some of the most celebrated figures in Indian stage and screen will be in attendance. Their presence will not only elevate the artistic caliber of the festival but also offer aspiring performers an extraordinary opportunity to learn from their journeys through workshops, discussions, and interactions.

Rashtriya Natya Mahotsav is more than just a festival—it is a cultural movement aimed at strengthening the theatrical fabric of the region. By creating a platform for dialogue, mentorship, and performance, the festival aspires to support emerging talent, celebrate artistic diversity, and build a thriving theatre ecosystem for the future.
